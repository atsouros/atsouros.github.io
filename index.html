Education

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at the Laboratoire de Physique de l'École Normale Supérieure (LPENS). Previously, I was an HFRI fellow at the Institute of Astrophysics - FORTH, in Heraklion, Crete, Greece, where I completed my PhD under the supervision of Vasiliki Pavlidou. I completed my MSc at the Mathematical Institute, University of Oxford, and my BSc at the Physics Department, University of Crete.
